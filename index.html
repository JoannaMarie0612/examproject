BIOGRAPHY


Jonas is an original, unique artist who started playing on different kind of instruments at the age of 5 and has been following his passion since then. He never took music lessons as his passion motivated him enough to learn everything by himself. He puts more focus on composing music , but beside that he is extremely creative and has a natural talent in drawing, animating, acting, singing, writing. What is special about him is he naturally feels the art and it comes deeply from his heart. When people ask him why he is doing what he is doing he just simply says "I want to tell a story". He gives a meaning to his music and the audience might take that with themselves in a different way. They translate the music to their own language giving their own meaning to the song.
